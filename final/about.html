Meet Our Guides

Alex Chen

Regions served: Asia, North America

Alex loves to travel and try new things! He is knowledgeable in both Asian and North American history and geography. When he's not guiding, he loves to ski and skateboard. He lives in Omaha with his dog, Cherry.

Alice Gray

Regions served: Europe, Oceania

Alice grew up in a family that lived in various places around the world, and she doesn't like to settle in one place too long! She is passionate about food and art. When she's not guiding, she loves to play the piano and do pilates. She lives in New York City with a houseplant named Harry.

Arjun Sharma

Regions served: Asia, Africa & the Middle East

Arjun started his adult life behind a desk, and quickly realized that he'd rather be traveling. He loves to game and play pickleball. He lives in Salt Lake City with his wife Priya and his parakeet Sunny.

Elizabeth Grant

Regions served: Oceania

Elizabeth hails from Sydney, Australia and loves to show people her favorite places. When she's not guiding, she trains for marathons and paints watercolor landscapes. She lives with her husband and son.

Jason Lee

Regions served: Asia, Europe

Jason loves to travel, but he also loves to be at home. His favorite city to guide is Bangkok and he tries a new street food every time he visits. He is an avid reader and baker. He lives in Paris with his wife and their cat, Gibbs.

Jeremy Lucas

Regions served: North America, Oceania

Jason is an avid outdoorsman and guides our hiking and camping tours. Even when he's home in Denver, he's likely outdoors fishing or tending to his yard. He was recently married to his wife, Alexis.

Wendy Graham

Regions served: North America, Africa & the Middle East

Wendy is our newest tour guide and you'll likely find her on the Canadian Railway Tour or leading a group around the Holy Land. She loves to knit and read, but her first love is travel. She lives with her cat Daphne in Toronto.
