Site Purpose

Visitors can peruse a catalog of available tours or fill out a form to get more information and join the mailing list.

Scenarios

  • What are the current tours available?
  • where can I get more information, like itineraries, about the tours listed?
  • Where can I sign up to be notified about future tours?
  • How can I join the mailing list to be notified about new tours and get great travel tips?
